Sniper Elite: Resistance - Out Now

The game I've been working on at Rebellion is now out!

Jan 30, 2025
3 minute read

Game Out

The game that I’ve been working on over at Rebellion is out today. We’ve worked very hard on it and I hope everyone that gets to play it enjoys it immensely.

Sniper Elite is a very important franchise for me. The first game I got to release in it was Sniper Elite 5, back in May 2022. Three years later, I get to show you and take part in the release of Sniper Elite: Resistance as well. This time around, its not Karl Fairburne, but Harry Hawker, who stars as the main protagonist.

Trailer

Watch the trailer of the game below.

Credits

I’ll drop an image from the credits of the game here, showing my name next to all of my incredibly skilled colleagues. I’ve been very lucky to get to work together with these people. Special thanks to everyone at Rebellion, but also to everyone at Wushu, who worked so hard on this game.

The names of my colleagues seen above:

Lead DLC Artist: Simon Ible

Environment Art Director: Jay Malhotra

Lead Lighting Artist: Forrest Pando

Art Team: Aaron Callander, Bryn Williams, Chi Loon Tang, Chris McWilliam, Frank Bowtell, Jacky Chim, Jade Mouflier, Jamie Johnson, Julianna Ip, Lewis J Barnett, Mingyo Choi, Paul Wright, Peter Dimitrov, Phil Cockburn, Rory Piper, Sam Buchanan, Sam Hipwell, Simon Richards, Steve Cronin

Technical Art Director: Delainey Ackerman

Technical Art Team: Chris Watts, James Morris, Zak Lloyd

Material Art Team: Pablo Sedano

There are lots and lots of others. You can see that by watching the full credits. The people above are some of the ones I got to work with directly. Once again, I’m very thankful for that.

Further Info

Sniper Elite: Resistance is out on Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, PlayStation 5 Pro, PlayStation 5 and PlayStation 4, Windows PC and PC via Steam and Epic Games Store.

If you wish to find out more, head over to the news article that my colleagues put up here: https://sniperelite.com/news/in-the-fight-sniper-elite-resistance-launches-today

It will give you all of the details on where to get the game, what it features and more.
